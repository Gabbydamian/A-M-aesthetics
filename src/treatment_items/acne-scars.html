Non-surgical rhinoplasty

Non-surgical nose reshaping is an incredibly popular treatment and can help to improve the aesthetic look of your nose. Whilst some people have a proportioned and symmetrical nose, many people aren’t content with the shape and other aspects of their nose.
Also known as a liquid nose job, nose re-shaping is a significantly less invasive and safe alternative to surgery, yielding fast results. It is particularly effective for making a nose appear straighter, eliminating bumps, edges and cavities.

Procedure

After a discussion with one of our Medical Aesthetic Doctors about your treatment expectations we will explain in detail the specifics of your individual case and answer all your questions. Then the procedure can go ahead. It’s a straightforward, fast and painless treatment.
The fillers work by adding volume to fill out irregularities across your nose such as bumps and edges, whilst also improving profile.
Treatment is performed with your unique facial aesthetic in mind, to give a natural, authentic and in-keeping result.

Results

A liquid nose job will result in improving issues like:
Raising the bridge of your nose
Correcting mild deviation
Refining its shape and increasing the projection of your nose’s tip Improve irregularities that have occurred through trauma or injury.
By achieving balance and harmony on the face with non-surgical nose re-shaping, many people find they feel more comfortable in their skin, which has a knock-on effect in other areas of their lives.

One of the main benefits of non-surgical nose re-shaping is there is no need to arrange for time off work. You can leave the clinic and resume your normal activities immediately after the procedure.

After around two weeks you will see the full results of nose re-shaping, with the filler settling into its final position. You can expect the effects to last for up to a year, though regular treatment can prolong the effects beyond this time.

You may experience some mild swelling for a maximum of two weeks after the procedure. Mild bruising is also possible, but not common, for a few hours after the treatment.
